Kiss, Marry, Kill

Rules:

This is the game of Pokemon: Kiss, Marry, Kill. Click and drag ONE Pokemon into each of these 3 categories:

Kiss: You're okay having one kiss with this Pokemon. You could see yourself going on a nice date date with them, but nothing more than that.

Marry: You could spend the rest of your life with this Pokemon. You could buy a house, have three beautiful children with them, and retire to a condo in Florida with them.

Kill: You could kill this Pokemon in cold blood. No second thoughts at all.
